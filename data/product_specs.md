# E-Shop Product Specifications

## Discount Codes
- Valid discount code "SAVE15" applies 15% discount to total order
- Valid discount code "WELCOME10" applies 10% discount to total order
- Discount codes are case-insensitive
- Only one discount code can be applied per order
- Discount applies before shipping costs

## Shipping Methods
- Standard shipping: Free
- Express shipping: $10.00
- Shipping method selection is required

## Cart Functionality
- Maximum quantity per item: 10
- Minimum quantity per item: 1
- Cart should update total price automatically when quantities change
- Items can be removed from cart

## Form Validation
- Email must be in valid format (user@domain.com)
- All fields in User Details form are required
- Name field: minimum 2 characters, maximum 50 characters
- Email field: maximum 100 characters
- Address field: minimum 10 characters, maximum 200 characters

## Payment Methods
- Credit Card
- PayPal
- Payment method selection is required
